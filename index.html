Welcome to Curiousers!


Curiousers is a TeamSpeak server for gamers who love to play, chat and have fun. Whether you are into FPS, RPG, MOBA, strategy or any other genre, you will find fellow gamers here who share your passion and are ready to team up with you.

Curiousers is a cool and relaxed community that welcomes everyone, regardless of their skill level, platform or preferences. We have a few simple rules to ensure a fun and fair gaming experience for all:

  • Be friendly and supportive to other users.
  • Do not cheat, grief, rage or troll anyone.
  • Do not share any illegal or inappropriate content.
  • Use the appropriate channels for your games.
  • Have fun and be curious!

Join Curiousers

If you want to join Curiousers, you will need to download and install the TeamSpeak client from here. Then, you can connect to our server using the following details:

Server name:Curiousers (or whatever you want)
