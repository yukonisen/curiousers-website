Curiousers!


Curiousers is a community for gamers who love to play, chat and have fun. Whether you are into FPS, RPG, MOBA, strategy or any other genre, you will find fellow gamers here who share your passion and are ready to team up with you.

Teamspeak 3

If you want to join Curiousers, you will need to download and install the TeamSpeak 3 client from here. Then, you can connect to our server using the following details:

Server name:Curiousers (or whatever you want)
Server address:ts.curiousers.org
Server password:staro
